Suggested Tour Plan

  1. Historic Tour
    • Take the ferry to Liberty Island. There you can visit the Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island. Afterwards visit downtown Manhattan to visit South Street Seaport and reflect on the September 11th memorial at Ground Zero.
  2. Observation
    • Visit the breathtaking views from Rockefeller Center: Top of the Rock Observation Deck or the tallest building of New York City: One World Observatory.
  3. Museums
    • Visit the Metropolitan Museum of Art to enjoy over 5,000 years of art from around the world.
